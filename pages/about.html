3-D NETWORK MAPS

Visualizing the evolution of human networks

About

In collaboration with UNC’s Digital Humanities Lab, this project’s goal is to map fairly complex human networks and their evolution over time using 3D maps to be viewed in web browsers or in VR. By formulating a node geometry that can clearly represent datasets with many variables, the tool is meant to help researchers and thinkers in the humanities gather new insights.
